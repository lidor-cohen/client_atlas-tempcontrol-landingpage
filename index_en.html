Advanced Temperature Control

Atlas Systems presents the T-Log 4X4 series, a cellular temperature and humidity recorder for complete control - the most advanced and innovative system in Israel for temperature monitoring

Temperature and humidity monitoring system for cooling and freezing, for trucks and refrigerated vehicles at controlled temperature, medicine refrigerators, food and agricultural produce. Combining the Atlas system with a T-log series recorder allows you to gain complete control over the cooling status at any given moment.

Monitor any change in real-time, and set up alerts to email and mobile phone. A cellular temperature recorder that complies with Israeli Standard 1291 for refrigerated food transport, and is suitable for Procedure 126 for the transport of medical preparations. The recorder operates on 4G and 5G networks according to the Ministry of Communications regulations.
